Harriette Wilson, née Dubouchet [married name Rochfort], English courtesan.

To John Anthony Galignani, bookseller – 5 letters:

- **Letter 1**: Autograph letter unsigned – *"Saturday night"* [1825?]  
  (MISC 3405), 3 pages (double sheet), from No. 111 Faubourg St Honoré  
  Begins: *"Mr Rochfort sent you all the plates yet published by your own order after he had acquainted you with the price..."*

- **Letter 2**: Autograph letter signed "Hariette Rochfort" – [26 Sep 1825]  
  (MISC 3406), 1 page (double sheet), from No. 91 Rue de Chaillot, Champs Élysées  
  Begins: *"We have the 10 11 12 part of the memoirs encluding Stockdales and my own reply to abusive editors of English news papers..."*

- **Letter 3**: Autograph letter signed "Hariette Rochfort" – 11 Oct [1825?]  
  (MISC 3407), 1 ½ pages (double sheet), from 14 Rue Colynee [sic] [?] St Honoré  
  Begins: *"I am only in Paris for a few days – I have just got the 13th part of my memoirs from London..."*

- **Letter 4**: Autograph letter signed "Hariette Rochfort" – [27 Jun 1828]  
  (MISC 3408), 2 ¼ pages (double sheet), from [Hô]tel des Languedociennes, Rue St Roch Poissonnière, No. 12  
  Begins: *"I have some very fine maps of South America Mexico which MacGregor had collected with much care..."*

- **Letter 5**: Autograph letter signed "Hariette Rochfort" – 21 Feb [circa 1828?]  
  (MISC 3409), 1 page (single sheet), from 91 Grande Rue de Chaillot, Paris  
  Begins: *"I only required and asked for next Sundays Paris Observer and I see you have put me down six months?"*
