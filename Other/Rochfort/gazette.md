nee's estate and interest therein, upon certain terms to be
submitted to the meeting, and in the event of the same
proposal being declined, to determine whether any other
and what terms, having respect to the said estate and
interest and a release of the said insolvent's interest therein,
or of the said assignees in his right or otherwise, should be
offered to such parties so claiming to be entitled as afore-
said, or whether any and what suits or other proceedings
should be taken by the said assignees to establish their own
claims on the said property, and to authorize the said
assignees to adopt or to compromise the same, and to act
therein as they shall be advised or consider necessary or
expedient, and on other special affairs.

All Letters must be Post-paid.
